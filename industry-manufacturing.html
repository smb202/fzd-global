— Common business problems

What manufacturing leaders bring to us.

Recurring patterns we see across cement, steel, packaging, automotive and chemicals. Operations data is rich; the challenge is connecting the shop floor to management reporting.

Problems we solve

  • Machine data disconnected from reporting
  • Production KPIs not standardized
  • Maintenance data not used predictively
  • ERP and shop-floor systems separated
  • Quality data scattered across tools
  • Group-level visibility delayed by weeks

Solutions we build

  • Production dashboards (line, plant, group)
  • IoT analytics on machine telemetry
  • Predictive maintenance signals
  • ERP / MES / shop-floor integration
  • Operations cockpit for plant managers
  • Group-level performance reporting

Example KPIs

  • OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)
  • Throughput, scrap and rework rate
  • Unplanned downtime, MTTR / MTBF
  • Maintenance cost per unit
  • Energy consumption per output
  • On-time delivery rate

Packaging Industry · 2023–2024

Five-country ERP consolidation in packaging

Azure · MS Fabric · Databricks · Power BI

Built a cloud-based DWH consolidating ERP systems from five countries. Migrated on-premise SSIS to Azure Data Factory. Connected SAP BW, HANA and Qlik Sense. Single point of truth for the executive team across the entire group.

Group reporting cycle cut from 9 to 3 days. Audit-ready lineage end-to-end.
