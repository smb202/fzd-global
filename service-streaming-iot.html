Real-Time Streaming & IoT Analytics

When latency is the product — fraud detection, predictive maintenance, live operations — batch is not an option. We design streaming architectures for bursty IoT telemetry.

Overview

When latency is the product — fraud detection, predictive maintenance, live operations — batch is not an option. We design streaming architectures for bursty IoT telemetry.

"Real-Time Streaming & IoT Analytics is not a product purchase — it is an architectural commitment. We help leadership teams make that commitment with their eyes open."

How it works

  • 01Event ingestion

    Kafka, Event Hubs or Kinesis sized for peak load. Schema registries and contracts.

  • 02Stream processing

    Stateful processing with exactly-once semantics. Windowing, joins and aggregations declared.

  • 03Time-series storage

    InfluxDB, Azure Data Explorer or Timescale. Partitioned for query patterns, retention tiered for cost.

  • 04Real-time visualization

    Grafana for operational, Power BI for analytical. Both fed from the same source of truth.
