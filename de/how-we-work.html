— Delivery methodology

From discovery to continuous improvement, in seven defined steps.

Every engagement follows the same disciplined sequence — adapted to your scope and timeline. Senior consultants on the work from day one. No junior-heavy delivery, no slide-deck handoffs.

— The process

Seven steps. Documented outcomes at every milestone.

Each step has a defined deliverable, a clear owner, and a documented decision point before moving forward.

STEP 01

Discovery

Understand business goals, users, systems, KPIs and pain points. Map who owns what, who decides what, and what's hurting most.

  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Current-state systems & reports inventory
  • Business goals and success criteria
Typical: 1–2 weeks
STEP 02

Assessment

Review data sources, architecture, data quality, manual processes, costs and risks. Written assessment with concrete recommendations.

  • Architecture & data-source review
  • Data-quality assessment
  • Cost & risk analysis
Typical: 2–3 weeks
STEP 03

Solution Design

Define target architecture, dashboard concepts, automation logic, AI components and governance model — tied to business outcomes, not technology launches.

  • Reference architecture
  • KPI / dashboard specification
  • Governance & ownership model
Typical: 2–4 weeks
STEP 04

Prototype

Build a working proof of value quickly, on real data, so stakeholders see and validate the solution before full-scale build begins.

  • Working prototype on real data
  • Stakeholder review & iteration
  • Go / no-go decision
Typical: 3–4 weeks
STEP 05

Implementation

Hands-on delivery: pipelines, data models, dashboards, cloud infrastructure, automation flows and AI components. Documented to enable handover.

  • Pipelines & cloud infrastructure
  • Dashboards & semantic models
  • Automation & AI components
Typical: 8–16 weeks
STEP 06

Training & Handover

Documentation, user training, runbooks for operations teams, and clearly defined ownership for every component built.

  • Architecture & runbook documentation
  • End-user & admin training
  • Ownership transition
Typical: 1–2 weeks
STEP 07

Continuous Improvement

New KPIs, additional automation, performance optimization and governance refinement — typically delivered as small, predictable monthly engagements rather than another big-bang project.

  • Quarterly KPI & performance review
  • Incremental dashboard / pipeline improvements
  • Governance refinement & new use-case onboarding
Ongoing: monthly engagement model
— Operating principles

Three commitments that shape every engagement.

These are not posters. They define how we scope, staff and judge every project.

Senior consultants, on the work.

Every engagement is led by experienced consultants who do the work themselves — not a sales-led handoff to a junior team. The architect who designs your solution is the architect who builds it.

Architecture before implementation.

The cheapest mistake is the one we never make. We invest in design, governance and decision rationale before code, so implementation is fast and reversible.

Make ourselves unnecessary.

Documentation, training, and clear handover are scoped into every engagement. We aim for your internal team to operate, extend and own the solution after we leave.
