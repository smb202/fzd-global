— Common business problems

What energy and utility leaders bring to us.

Recurring patterns we see in renewable energy, grid operations and utility companies. Real-time sensor data is abundant; turning it into operational insight is the gap.

Problems we solve

  • Real-time sensor data not integrated
  • Asset performance unclear at portfolio level
  • Forecasting for demand and generation difficult
  • Operational alerts missing or noisy
  • Maintenance scheduling reactive
  • Cost allocation across sites unclear

Solutions we build

  • IoT analytics platform for asset telemetry
  • Energy operations dashboard
  • Demand and generation forecasting models
  • Real-time alert and threshold rules
  • Predictive maintenance signals
  • Cloud data platform with governance

Example KPIs

  • Asset uptime, capacity factor
  • Energy production / forecast accuracy
  • Network losses, transmission efficiency
  • Maintenance cost per MW
  • Demand forecast deviation
  • CO₂ intensity per site

Cloud-based IoT and data platform
