Overview

Automation is not about replacing people. It is about removing the work humans hate and machines do better — so humans can do the work humans are still better at.

"Process Automation & Intelligent Workflows is not a product purchase — it is an architectural commitment. We help leadership teams make that commitment with their eyes open."

How it works

  • 01Process mapping

    Existing flows captured end-to-end. Pain points and exceptions documented. Automation candidates ranked by ROI.

  • 02Power Platform

    Power Automate flows, Power Apps for UI, Power Pages for external surfaces. Connected to Microsoft 365, ERPs and any API.

  • 03RPA where APIs do not exist

    UiPath, Power Automate Desktop. Used surgically — RPA is a bandage, not a cure.

  • 04Citizen development

    Frameworks, templates and governance so business teams build their own automations safely.
