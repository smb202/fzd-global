— Technology stack

The technology stack we work with.

Pragmatic choices, not buzzword bingo. We pick technologies for the specific problem at hand — bias toward Microsoft Azure and Fabric for DACH enterprise environments, with best-of-breed alternatives where they fit.

— Stack categories

Six categories. Senior expertise across all.

Each tool earns its place in the stack only after it's proven its value in production engagements.

Cloud & Engineering

Tool selection is part of the engagement.

We don't sell tools. Tool choice is a deliverable: a written recommendation tied to your scale, team skill profile, regulatory environment and TCO. If you already have an investment in Microsoft, Databricks or Snowflake, we work with what you have — and only recommend change when the evidence is overwhelming.
