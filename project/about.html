— Founder & practice

A boutique data &
AI consultancy
built around one principle.

FZD Global is led by Faruk Zorlu — Senior Data & AI Solutions Architect, founder, and the senior architect on every engagement we take. The principle: senior people, on the work, every day. Not a sales motion that ends with a junior team.

I started building enterprise data systems in 2014, working through banking, insurance, energy, manufacturing and medical technology before founding FZD Global to do the work the way I always wanted to do it: with senior architects, in close collaboration with leadership, and with a bias toward outcomes that survive the consultant leaving.

Over the past decade I have led architectures across SAP BW, IBM Cognos, Microsoft Fabric, Azure Synapse, Databricks, Snowflake, Power BI and the modern AI stack. The technologies change. The discipline does not: understand the business, model the data honestly, ship something useful, instrument it, iterate.

FZD Global serves DACH-region clients in five core sectors. Engagements range from focused architecture sprints to multi-year strategic partnerships. Whatever the shape — I am on every one.

— Operating principles

Three commitments that shape the work.

These are not posters. They are how engagements are scoped, staffed and judged.

01

Senior on the work.

You buy our practice, you get our practice. No bait-and-switch to junior teams. Every engagement has a senior architect doing the work — not just attending the steerco.

02

Outcome over output.

Anyone can ship slides and deliverables. We measure ourselves on what changes after we leave: faster close cycles, better forecasts, decisions made on data instead of intuition.

03

Boutique by choice.

We say no to engagements that need 30 consultants. The work we take is the work that benefits from depth and continuity, not headcount. That keeps quality up and senior attention undiluted.
