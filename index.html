01 — Problems we solve

Six problems. All fixable.

Same root causes, different symptoms. The right architecture and governance resolves each one.

PROBLEM 01

Reporting is manual and slow.

Automated dashboards, governed KPIs and reliable refresh pipelines that replace month-end Excel marathons with daily, trusted numbers.

PROBLEM 02

Data sits in disconnected systems.

Cloud data warehouses, lakehouses, APIs and integration pipelines that bring ERP, CRM, IoT and Excel into one analytics-ready model.

PROBLEM 03

Management does not trust the numbers.

Data-quality rules, KPI definitions, ownership models and governance — so the dashboard your CFO opens on Monday is the one source of truth.

PROBLEM 04

AI projects stay as demos.

Production-ready RAG systems, agents and forecasting models with evaluation frameworks that survive past the proof-of-concept stage.

PROBLEM 05

Dashboards exist, but nobody uses them.

Executive-focused dashboard redesign, adoption logic and performance optimization — moving users out of Excel and into the report.

PROBLEM 06

Cloud and BI costs keep growing.

Architecture review, platform simplification and cost optimization — typically removing 20–40% of unused capacity in the first 60 days.
