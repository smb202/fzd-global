03 — How we work

Senior consultants. From whiteboard to production.

FZD Global is built on the conviction that strategy without delivery is a slide deck, and delivery without strategy is technical debt. Our consultants do both.

STEP 01

Diagnose

Architecture review, stakeholder interviews and a written assessment with concrete next steps. Two to four weeks.

STEP 02

Design

Reference architecture, governance model, and roadmap with milestones tied to business outcomes — not technology launches.

STEP 03

Build

Hands-on delivery. Cloud platform, pipelines, models, dashboards, automation. Documented to enable internal teams to take over.

STEP 04

Enable

Workshops, training, runbooks and CI/CD. We aim to make ourselves unnecessary on every engagement.
