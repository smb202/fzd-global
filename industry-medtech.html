— Common business problems

What healthcare and MedTech leaders bring to us.

Recurring patterns we see across diagnostic centers, hospitals, MedTech operators and care providers. Each is fixable with the right architecture and operational analytics.

Problems we solve

  • Device utilization unclear across sites
  • Manual appointment and capacity planning
  • Clinical and operational data separated
  • Cancellations and blocked slots not analyzed
  • EHR, IoT, ERP and scheduling not connected
  • Management reports arrive too late

Solutions we build

  • Medical operations dashboard
  • Device capacity cockpit
  • Predictive calendar & load forecasting
  • EHR / IoT / ERP integration layer
  • Clinical KPI reporting
  • Operational alert systems
  • Staff & capacity analytics

Example KPIs

  • Device utilization, weekly examination volume
  • Appointment cancellations & blocked slots
  • Patient volume, waiting time
  • Staff capacity & load

MS Fabric Data Mesh and Lakehouse

MS Fabric · Power BI · Power Apps · Power Automate · Jedox
